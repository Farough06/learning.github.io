Bienvenu a L'Association Suivi Enfants de Gabalan

The Suivi Enfant association aims to support the educational community in the care of different children: families, teachers, AESH and other professionals. The Suivi Enfant association is run by members who have significant experience of the school system, establishments, structures and the operation of support systems. Rigorous monitoring in terms of digital, technological and scientific advances is put in place. The Suivi Enfant association participates in events, organizes demonstrations, training, etc.
